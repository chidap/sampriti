Covid Guidelines for Durga Puja

  • At the moment, the puja is planned only for members, donors and sponsors; No walk in visitors will be allowed inside the premise.
  • All visitors need to be screened with Thermal Gun at the entrance. Vistiros having temperature of 38 degree C or more will not be allowed to enter.
  • All vistors need to record their contact details at the entrance (Aadhar, DI, Voters ID, Office ID with contact number).
  • All vistors need to sanitize their hand and enter the hall earing MASK. No permission for entry of visitors without MASK and sanitization.
  • Marking of areas for people to stand with adequate physical distance of at least 1 meter. Frequent sanitization of pandal, stalls and dinning hall.
  • Rituals like Pushpanjali, Sondhi pujo, will be performed by maintaining adequate physical distancing. To avoid crowding - Batch wise pushpanjali; per batch 20-25 people
  • No Sindur Khela this year.No cut fruits to be offered for puja. At all time, the visitors should be wearing MASKS.
  • At the dinning hall adequate distance to be maintained between each table and chairs. Only packed food will be served; if additional quantity required, then additional packets will be provided.
