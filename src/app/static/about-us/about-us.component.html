About Us

Chennai Sampriti Cultural Association (CSCA), as the name suggest, is an association of people in Greater Chennai, whose mother tongue is Bengali - or who are otherwise interested in the heritage of Bengal. Our effort lies in bringing together the Bengali community in celebrating our diverse cultural heritage.

At Chennai Sampriti, we build and foster friendship and understanding between the people through the promotion of traditional and modern art forms, including (but not limited to) language, customs, dress, food, music, dance, poetry, drama, literature.

We are registered non-profit, tax-exempt 501(c)(3) organization.

  • Promote socio-cultural values
  • Give respect to individual believe and culture
  • 100% non-political and non-profit
